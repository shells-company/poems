## About this collection

These poems were written between quiet hours and restless ones. Some came together in a single sitting; others took weeks to find their shape. They're grouped loosely by mood rather than chronology — read them in any order.

## How to read

- **Take your time.** Most pieces are short, but they breathe better with a pause between lines.
- **Read aloud if you can.** Rhythm and breath matter more than meaning, sometimes.
- **Revisit.** A poem you brushed past one week might land differently the next.

## A note from the author

Thanks for stopping by. If something here resonates — or doesn't — I'd love to hear about it. Writing into the quiet is a strange thing; knowing someone's on the other side makes it less so.
