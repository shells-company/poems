The man in the mirror is waving at me. He's coming clearer.\
Cleaning glasses as a metaphor for reflecting more.\
I'm hanging up my clothes.\
Moving them out of the closet.\
Putting them on a rack that I can see everything that makes me me.

> Mirrors.

> I can sit down on the bus.\
> I don't always have to be the one suffering the most.\
> I can rest.

I used to be afraid of bees.\
But they won't hurt me.\
They just want to get back home to their Queen.\
Just like me.\
They long to be...

I'm brushing my hair more.

I like how I look.\
I'm older now.\
I care about how I look.

The man in the mirror is standing taller now.\
I didn't notice that.\
Did the mirror adjust my posture?

> Ok that's enough cleaning. If I clean everything what would I do in my free time?

The skill of reflection is hanging yourself on a wall and framing it\
If I don't die I can't be hung\
If I can't be hung I can't be seen
