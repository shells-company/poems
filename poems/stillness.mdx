---
title: "Stillness"
description: "Finding quiet in the noise."
sidebarTitle: "Stillness"
---

There's a moment right before the world wakes up,\
where everything is yours.

No notifications.\
No expectations.\
Just you and the hum of something ancient.

I used to be afraid of the quiet,\
thought it meant I was falling behind.\
Now I know —\
the stillness is where I catch up to myself.

Let the world spin without me for a second.\
I'll be back.\
I always come back.
