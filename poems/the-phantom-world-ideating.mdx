This was never a problem when the real world felt great.\
The real world was far greater than my fantasies.\
There was so much to look forward to.\
So many things to do.\
And an ultimate goal.\
That felt unattainable.\
,a "but maybe." if you will.\
Maybe I could give up all control to someone I can truly trust.\
maybe that is what will make me happy.

To expose myself and feel like I am accepted.

---

My fantasies are great, wonderful and they feel true.

yet how much time have I wasted. Living in this phantom world.

I create great things while I'm in the phantom world,\
they reflect so much of me.\
When the phantom world is cooking,\
the real world is second place.

The real world is just a loading zone,\
the fantasies are all the memories.

I hear about these phantom worlds.\
Through books of books.\
They are actually quite unique.

The real world is just a loading zone,\
the fantasies are all the memories.

But when I wake up my only question.\
how much time was spent in this fantasy
