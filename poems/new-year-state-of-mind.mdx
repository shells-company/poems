_My thoughts trying to finish a side project website before the New Year. I was beyond exhausted from thinking about and telling my friends "its coming soon". I listened to a lot of "Nas" — New York State of Mind while working on this._

---

Monday, Dec 29th 2025

I'm exhausted\
Yet I can't rest

> I never sleep cause sleep is the cousin of death.

All the 13 reasons why still exist.\
I have to cross off one item before the day ends,\
at least before the me of 2025 dies.

I only have 4 days to live.\
Why waste that life on sleeping.\
Shouldn't I want something more than just dreams and unconsciousness

I should take a break if I'm so exhausted and stressed\
I can't take a break, the list still exists,\
I can't finish the list because I'm exhausted and stressed.

I hate when I get stuck in these cycles.

> I lay puzzled as I backtrack to earlier times\
> Nothing's equivalent to the New York state of mind
