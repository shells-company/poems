description: "On exhaustion and cycles."
sidebarTitle: "Unproductive"
---

I'm exhausted.\
yet I can't rest.\
the list remains unchecked.\
All the 13 reasons why still exist.

I have to finish it.\
before the day ends\
before the me of today dies.

Yet I'm exhausted, I need to rest.\
How many more days can I do this for.

I can't take a break, because the list exists.\
I can't finish because I'm exhausted and stressed.

I hate cycles.

The more I hold to it,\
the more I get bitten.
