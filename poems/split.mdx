I made a reality he doesn't know about\
My own little safe haven\
I hide letters in my pockets\
with no postmark date\
In case he want to see me again.

He might find one of these,\
to see me again\
or I might\
years later\
wearing that jacket\
I loved and stopped myself from wearing\
reach into the inside pocket and touch paper\
like winning the jackpot\
will it ruin my life?\
will it save me?\

it's what I do\
